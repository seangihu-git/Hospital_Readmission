### Research Question
⭐ Is there a relationship between patient experience scores and excess hospital readmission rates?

### Objective
🚩 To identify opportunities for reducing hospital penalties and costs while improving overall quality of patient care.

### Methodology
⚙️ Hospital general information, readmission data, and HCAHPS patient survey data were cleaned and integrated into a unified dataset. The combined data was analyzed and visualized using Tableau to explore trends and relationships. 

### Key Findings
🔎 The analysis revealed a negative correlation between patient experience ratings and excess readmission rates — hospitals with higher patient satisfaction tend to have lower readmission rates. 

### Recommendations
👩🏻‍⚕️ To reduce excess readmissions, hospitals should focus on improving patient experience metrics, particularly in areas with lower ratings. This can be achieved by:

- Identifying underperforming categories and subcategories
- Benchmarking against high-performing facilities
- Investing in staff training and development to enhance quality of care
